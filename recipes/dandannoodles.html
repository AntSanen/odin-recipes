Dan Dan Noodles

Description

If you grave some real good asian noodles this recipe is just perfect.

Dan Dan Noodles are fast, easy to make and incredible delicious!

Ingredients

Sauce
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbs chilli flakes
  • sesame seeds
  • 2 ts sichuan pepper
  • 4 tbs oil
  • 2 tbs brown sugar
  • 4 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tbs peanutbutter
  • s ts rice vinegar
Noodles
  • 200g minced beef
  • pak choi
  • noodles of your choice
  • spring onions, diced

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pot till it shows streaks. Meanwhile mix the garlic, chilli flakes, sesame seeds and the sichuan pepper in a bowl
  2. When oil is hot enough pour over the ingredients in the bowl and after some mixing add the rest of sauce ingredients
  3. stir fry the beef in a pan and when it's dark and crumbly add the pak choi for a few minutes
  4. cook noodles. Add some cooking water to the sauce and divide evrything into two bowl. Add the sauce and mix. In the end top it of with some spring onion
