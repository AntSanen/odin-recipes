Lasagna

Description

A good bubbely lasagna is widely known as one of the best comfort dishes to exist, but how to make this beloved pasta dish?

I'll tell you below

Ingredients

  • 500g pound ground beef
  • 2 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 800g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbs tomatoe paste
  • 500 ml milk
  • 2 tbs flour
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 200g gouda cheese
  • one package of Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. In a large pan brown the beef with the onions and garlic over medium-high heat
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, italian seasoning and pepper. Let everything simmer for 45min
  3. In a different pan melt the butter and ad the flour, after it thickens add the milk and season to taste. Let it simmer until it thickens
  4. Shred the cheese and begin stacking the Lasagna after the sauces are done
  5. Top the Lasagna of with the shredded gouda and pop in the ofen for 45min on 175C.
